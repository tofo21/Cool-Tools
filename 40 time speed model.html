τ (tau) is the time constant controlling how quickly an athlete reaches peak speed. Lower τ = more explosive off the line. Higher τ = top-end speed carries the time. NFL combine average: ~0.77s (Clark et al., J Strength Cond Res 2019). Worthy 4.21 → 24.41 mph confirmed by NFL Next Gen Stats.
